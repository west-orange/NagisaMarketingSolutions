About Us

Founded in 2024, A Little Bit of Japan was created with the vision of bringing the essence of Japan to India in meaningful and accessible ways. We act as a cultural bridge, helping individuals and organizations experience Japanese values, aesthetics, and traditions within the Indian context.

Through our expertise in Business development, corporate engagement, and brand engagement, we design impactful solutions that foster cross-cultural understanding and collaboration. Guided by integrity, execution excellence, and creativity, we are committed to delivering programs that connect people, inspire growth, and build lasting cultural resonance.
