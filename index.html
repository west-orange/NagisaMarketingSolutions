Mission

About Us

Founded in 2024, NAGISA MARKETING SOLUTIONS was created with the vision of bringing the essence of Japan to India in meaningful and accessible ways. We act as a cultural bridge, helping individuals and organizations experience Japanese values, aesthetics, and traditions within the Indian context.

Through our expertise in Business development, corporate engagement, and brand engagement, we design impactful solutions that foster cross-cultural understanding and collaboration. Guided by integrity, execution excellence, and creativity, we are committed to delivering programs that connect people, inspire growth, and build lasting cultural resonance.

Team

Nagisa Iizuka

Founder, CEO

She first visited India in 2015 and later received the MEXT "Tobitate! Study Abroad Japan" scholarship, gaining hands-on experience through internships including an Indian startup. After working in corporate sales in Japan's IT startup and venture sector, she founded Nagisa Marketing Solutions in Mumbai in 2024. She works closely with Japanese companies in India, collaborating with Indian-led teams to enhance Japan's presence and support organizational growth.

Gagandeep Makkar

Japanese Culture Consultant, Trainer

With over 10 years of experience, she specializes not only in teaching the Japanese language but also in delivering impactful cross-cultural sessions bridging India and Japan. Her background includes government projects, interpretation/translation, and serving as Senior Cross-Cultural Integration Trainer at Pasona India. She provides deep insight into Japanese communication styles and business etiquette. JLPT N1 certified.
